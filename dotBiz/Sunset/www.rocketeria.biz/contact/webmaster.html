Contact the Webmaster

Construction & maintenance of this site is performed by friends of Rocketeria with kids, spouses, day jobs, and other personal endeavors (namely music!). We hardly expect it to be perfect, but we do expect it to look decent, function as intended, and be at least as secure as any other hack job web app.

 

If parts of it look funny or don't work as you think they should, we would be grateful to hear from you. If there is a security issue putting us or our visitors at risk, we would be grateful to hear from you. We have a generous bug bounty program, payable in your choice of hugs, high-fives, or, if you're really nice, a round of drinks at a fine local establishment.
