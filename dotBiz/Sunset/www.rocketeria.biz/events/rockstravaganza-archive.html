ROCKSTRAVAGANZA 2017

Live Music Showcase & Fundraiser

 

 

Sunday January 8, 2017 @ 6PM

Sandy Spring Friends School
Performing Arts Center

 

Tickets $15 at the door

 

 

 

Rockstravaganza is our annual live performance showcase, an opportunity for select Jam Groups,  teachers and staff to show off their musical skills and talents. This year's show took place at Sandy Spring Friends School, in their state-of-the-art performing arts center. 

 

The show is back-to-back tunes, in a rock concert style. It's also a fundraiser, with proceeds going towards putting on our annual Olney Days Rock The Lot Block Party (taking place on May 21, 2017).

 

2017's show saw the return of Our Pal Pete, the famous Pete Wright, our awesome staff band, The Policy and these excellent bands born at Rocketeria:
