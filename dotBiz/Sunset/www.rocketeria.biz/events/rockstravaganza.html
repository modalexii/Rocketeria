ROCKSTRAVAGANZA 2018

Live Music Showcase & Fundraiser

 

Sunday February 11, 2018 @ 3PM

Union Jack's in Gaithersburg

 

 

That's right, this is not a ticketed event, but open to the public. Please help us raise funds by donating, instead!

 

 

Rockstravaganza is our annual live performance showcase, an opportunity for select Jam Groups,  teachers, star students and staff to show off their chops. This year's show will take place in a real rock club environment, on the pro Union Jack's stage, at the Rio in Gaithersburg.

 

It's a rock show! It's also a fundraiser, with proceeds going towards putting on our annual Olney Days Rock The Lot Block Party

2017 BAND PICTURES BY PATRICK RYAN

 

 

Pat Ryan's portraits of the Rockstravaganza bands may be found at http://imageryan.com/rockstravaganza2017.  From, there you can order prints as well as digital downloads of full-resolution images.  

 

Take care to note each image's aspect ratio when ordering prints.  Because of the varied poses, print ratios (e.g. 4x6, 8x10) will not necessarily line up with every image.  When you order a print, there is a "preview" option that will show you roughly what the final product will look like, including any cropping.http://imageryan.com/rockstravaganza2017

 

Images of the last four years of Rockstravaganza may be found at http://imageryan.com/rockstravaganza. 

 

If you have any questions about prints or photo enhancement (e.g. B&W conversion), please contact Pat directly at imageryan@gmail.com.
