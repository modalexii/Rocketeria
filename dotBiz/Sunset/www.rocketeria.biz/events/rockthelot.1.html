Party In The Park


***NEW FOR 2018***

DIFFERENT LOCATION! 

NOW AT OLNEY MANOR PARK BEFORE THE FIREWORKS
DIFFERENT DAY!

NOW ON SATURDAY APRIL 28, 2018

***NEW FOR 2018***

 

Join us for the innaugural "PARTY IN THE PARK" combining the 
12th Annual Rock The Lot Block Party with the Fun Fair & Fireworks. 

 

A party of the people, by the people, and for the people!

To benefit the Olney Civic Fund, Project Change, and the Sandy Spring Volunteer Fire Department, bring your whole family and neighbors to the Party In The Park!

 

Enjoy live music by our own great local bands:

Priceless Advice 6:50-8:00

The Hounds 5:35-6:35

Audio Fuel 4:20-5:20

So Close To Nothing 3:40-4:05

The Passarellas 3:00-3:25

 

Local craft beer and wine, lots of food trucks, carnival games for kids of all ages, and the tremendous Olney Big Band playing us in to the sunset and the most impressive of small town fireworks displays, exploding for the 40th year over our town.

 

Click here for the most up-to-date list of participants, activities, vendors, schedules and news.

 

Click here for the latest and greatest PARKING MAP

 

Help us put on the party this year, SATURDAY APRIL 28, 2018, by donating funds, exhibiting, or donating your time - we're looking for volunteers!

 

Please contact the Olney Civic Fund here today, to get involved or contribute.

 

 

The Rock The Lot Block Party's mission is to raise money for, and awareness of Olney's own award-winning and nationally recognized youth organization, 

Project Change, "a community-based nonprofit organization comprised of youth and adults who believe that civic-mindednes is essential to building and maintaining a strong, vibrant and healthy community". This is a value we whole-heartedly support, and are grateful to be able to contribute to, through working with this estimable organization!
