CURRENT STUDENTS
 

 

Anyone can and should play! Our students range in age from 2 to over 70. Learning to play an instrument is a brain-enhancing pursuit, good for folks of all ages. It matters not that you become the best, only that you enjoy the process, and the endless benefits throughout your life.

 

Our teaching philosophies are as varied as our talented staff but in general we think a student should learn for fun, should understand their equipment and how to care for it, and should pursue their individual goals at their own pace. Want to learn how to read music? Want to learn that new song you heard? Want to write and play your own stuff? Want to perform as part of a band? Sign up!
