Repairs

Rocketeria offers pro-level service 
for most instruments. 

 

While most work is performed on-site, our local specialty techs handle the more intricate jobs.

 

Estimates are free.

Guitar & Bass 

Restring

Includes clean & polish, machine head check.

  • Electric & Acoustic Guitar: $30 + Strings.
  • 12-String Guitar + $15.
  • Locking Tremolo System (Floyd Rose, Kahler, etc) + $20.
  • Tie-end Nylon Strings Guitar + $10.

 Setup

Includes clean & polish, complete hardware check, neck & bridge adjustments for improved action & intonation. Does not include electronic repair, nut/fret filing or parts replacement.

  • Acoustic: $45 + Strings. 12-String Guitar + $20.

Includes Neck adjust, tighten tuner screws and nuts, general clean up, restring. 

  • Electric: $60 + Strings. 12-String Guitar + $20. Locking Tremolo System (Floyd Rose, Kahler, etc) + $25.

Includes neck adjust, action and intonation adjustment at saddle, tighten tuner screws and nuts, general clean up, restring.

Electronics
