Dave Peers

 

Guitar, Bass

 

When did you start?

 

When I was 13, just like Ace Frehley.

 

Who are your influences?

 

See above, and Joe Perry/Brad Whitford, Rick Nielsen, Rich Robinson, and in hindsight, Eric Clapton. So many, really.

 

What's your musical background - where and who have you played with?

 

I played piano briefly before playing guitar, my family is musical but not musically obsessed (I took care of that, though.), played my first club when I was 16.

 

Notable bands played with include Nick Dastardly, The Excentrics,

Black Angel Down.

 

When did you first consider yourself a professional musician?

 

Don't know if I do to this day, but it seemed like the 90's was when I began to

make money by playing, mostly solo acoustic stuff.

 

Who do you admire?

 

My wife, Mom, kids, staff and customers!

 

What's your favorite part of teaching?

 

When the lesson's over and both the student and myself walk out

in a better mood than when we walked in.

 

Who are your favorite artists?

 

See #2, and also Elton John, Neil Diamond, Slash is huge for me these days

(his current stuff, not GNR so much).
