Andrew Bezila

 

Bass & Guitar + Beginner Piano, Drums & Ukulele

 

When did you start?

 

I began playing trombone in middle school, then I was playing upright bass in the orchestra and jazz ensemble by the end of middle school. My parents got me my first electric bass at age 12, and I’ve been playing that ever since.

 

Who are your influences?

 

Some of my influences these days are Sting, Charlie Parker, James Jamerson, Flea, Steve Harris, Billy Sheehan, Dee Dee Ramone and Roger Manganelli

 

What's your musical background—where and who have you played with?

 

I’ve probably played bass in your band or your friends band! I started off by playing along to punk CDs on my old boom box… Now I’ve played in wedding bands, jazz combos, R & B, Math Rock, Top 40, but what I really love to do is rock and jazz!


When did you first consider yourself a professional musician?

 

When I joined this wedding band that was getting paid to play huge events all over the place, and I was going to school for jazz simultaneously. I thought, yeah this is all I want to do!

 

Who do you admire? 

 

I admire anyone who puts in the time and effort to take their playing to the next level! I also admire people who are not afraid to be different. Also, I admire nice people! :)

 

What's your favorite part of teaching? 

 

My favorite part is helping people learn to express themselves on their chosen instrument, and develop a life long love for music.

 

Who are your favorite artists?

 

My tastes are constantly changing, but some of my current favorites are The Police, Iron Maiden, Herbie Hancock, Michael Jackson -- I love little Mike and the Jackson 5 -- The Ramones, Vulfpeck.
