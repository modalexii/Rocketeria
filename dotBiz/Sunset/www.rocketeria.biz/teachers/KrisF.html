Kris Fath

 

Voice

 

When did you start?

 

I came from a musical family, took piano lessons as a kid and always loved singing.

 

Who are your influences?

 

I have wide-ranging musical tastes but have always loved rock, soul and blues.

 

What's your musical background - where and who have you played with?

 

I played in my first band at 15 and have been playing ever since. 

 

I've played in Mystic Blue, the Peaches and Herb touring band, Wizzard, Orphan, Karma, and with Kristina Lucille. I'm currently performing regularly with Diamond Alley (27 years now!)

 

When did you first consider yourself a professional musician?

 

I started playing pretty regularly in bands at 15, and by the time I was 18 and had just started college, I was performing 6 nights a week.

 

Who do you admire?

 

Anyone who's pursued their craft and stuck with it through thick and thin, never throwing in the towel. Who figures out a way to continue pursuing their passion in a responsible way, who gives back.

 

What's your favorite part of teaching?

 

I enjoy seeing my students care enough to practice and improve,

and I enjoy their live performances!

 

Who are your favorite artists?
 

Janis Joplin, Bonnie Raitt, Chaka, Aretha, Heart, Beatles, early Zep and many others! Basically anyone passionate and good!
