Geoff Easthope

 

Drums, Band Snare, Percussion, Guitar (Beginner), Piano (Beginner)

 

When did you start?

 

I began playing when I was 11.

 

Who are your influences?

 

My early influences were The Who, Yes, Emerson Lake & Palmer, and King Crimson. Later, the west coast jazz scene, Tom Scott, Eric Gale, Richard Tee.

 

What's your musical background - where and who have you played with?

 

My first steady gig was at age 15 at a local Turkish restaurant.

When the belly dancer's tape broke I had to accompany her!

 

When did you first consider yourself a professional musician?

 

I turned pro at 17 straight out of high school playing dance clubs

at the height of the disco boom. Can't believe I'm still playing Good Times

by Chic after all these years!

 

Who do you admire?

 

Simon Phillips

 

What's your favorite part of teaching?

 

Best part of teaching is watching a student progress and becoming better

because of our combined efforts.

 

Who are your favorite artists?

 

Dave Weckl, Vinnie Colaiuta, Simon Phillips, Buddy Rich
