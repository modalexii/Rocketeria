ROCKETERIA, INC. MUSIC LESSONS POLICIES

Revised February 2, 2016

 

  • All lessons must be paid for in advance.  
  • You pay for all lessons whether you attend them or not. 
  • To discontinue, submit written notice by the 20th of the month, for the month to come. Verbal cancellations are not valid and tuition will continue to be charged until written notice is received.
  • Notice of cancellation of a lesson or absence must be given to a member of our counter staff, only. Such notice is invalid if delivered to your teacher.
  • Same day cancellations are ineligible for make up lessons.
  • Make up lessons expire 30 days after a cancelled lesson and are the customer's responsibility. Make up lessons cannot be rescheduled.
  • Lessons are NOT cancelled automatically when local school systems close.

TUITION, FEES & DISCOUNTS:

  • All lessons must be paid for in advance.
  • Payment for lessons is due in full regardless of attendance. Exceptions are at the discretion of the management. 
  • Tuition is payable only via credit card on file.
  • Should your card on file decline for any reason, we will send a courtesy email. Tuitions not paid by the first of the month will be subject to a $15 late fee. Our returned check fee is $35.
  • Tuition for months with holiday closings will be pro-rated. 
  1. WEEKLY students pay $41 per 30-minute private lesson, times the number of scheduled days per month. In other words, your monthly tuition varies according to "your" day-of-the-week; some months' tuition may be for 3, 4 or 5 days, for a monthly total of $123, $164, or $205. You are responsible for awareness of your monthly total.
  2. WEEKLY students' tuition is charged during the last week of the month and pays for the upcoming month in full, regardless of planned or unplanned student absences. You are pre-paying for the month, and reserving your day, time and teacher.
  3. WEEKLY students may purchase ADD-ON lessons (which are in addition to their regular weekly lesson with their regular teacher only) on an ad hoc basis, for $31 per 30-minute private lesson. ADD-ON lessons can also be reserved on a regularly scheduled weekly basis, by way of pre-payment for the month in advance, in conjunction with your tuition, subject to the same rules and restrictions as WEEKLY lessons.
  4. WEEKLY students' tuition is charged monthly on a continuous basis until notice of cancellation is received per the terms below. 
  5. WEEKLY students' tuition is payable only via credit card on file. Please refer to our credentials for details on your data security.
  6. WEEKLY students pay a one-time $25.00 registration fee per student ($50.00 maximum per family) upon enrollment, $15 if a returning student (after an absence of 12 months or less). This fee reserves your teacher, day and time on a continuous basis and covers costs for schedule changes, as described below.
  7. WEEKLY students' tuition is due for a full month, at the time of enrollment. If enrolling mid-month (defined as after the 15th), payment will be collected for the remainder of the month, plus the next full month.
  8. WEEKLY students are entitled to a 5% Family Discount on tuition when 2 or more immediate family members are enrolled as WEEKLY students.
  9. WEEKLY students receive a 10% Student Discount on most purchases at Rocketeria (exclusions and limitations apply). 
  10. PAYG (Pay-As-You-Go) and GROUP lesson students are not entitled to the Student Discount.
  11. PAYG students' lessons are $44 per 30-minute private lesson and must be paid for in full in advance to reserve the time. PAYG lessons cannot be rescheduled and are not eligible for refund. There is no registration fee.
  12. GROUP lessons are $31 per person (2 to 6 people) and must be paid for in full in advance to reserve the time. Group lessons cannot be rescheduled and are not eligible for a refund. There is no registration fee.

TO DISCONTINUE/RE-REGISTER: 

  1. To discontinue, submit written notice by the 20th of the month, for the month to come. For example, if you wish to stop taking lessons in the month of July, we must receive written notice by June 20.
  2. Such written notice must be given to a Rocketeria counter staff member (NOT the student's teacher) or sent by email to lessons@rocketeria.biz. 
  3. We assume that all WEEKLY students will continue lessons each month and tuition will continue to be charged until written notice is received by the 20th of the month. 
  4. Verbal cancellations are not valid and tuition will continue to be charged until written notice is received.
  5. Should you miss the cancellation deadline and tuition has already been charged, funds are non-refundable but will remain as store credit on your account, with no expiration.
  6. Students who re-register within 12 months of stopping lessons are charged a $15.00 re-activation fee. If more than 12 months have passed since the termination of the lessons, the standard $25.00 registration fee applies.


TO CANCEL/RESCHEDULE/MAKE UP LESSONS: 

  • You pay for your lessons whether you attend them or not. 
  • Only WEEKLY students may reschedule lessons, when at least 24 hours notice is given. ADD-ON lessons are also eligible, per the same terms.
  • Rescheduling lessons is a privilege. Your teacher is paid for your regularly scheduled/reserved time and offers their available times to make up a lesson, as a courtesy.
  • PAYG lessons may not be rescheduled.
  • GROUP lessons on a weekly or bi-monthly schedule may reschedule. "One-offs", no.
  1. Notice of cancellation of a lesson or absence must be given to a member of our counter staff, only. Such notice is invalid if delivered to your teacher.
  2. You may give such notice via email to lessons@rocketeria.biz, in person or via voice mail message to 301-774-5690.
  3. Only WEEKLY students' lessons are eligible for rescheduling, and rescheduling will only be considered if notice is received at least 24 hours in advance of the scheduled lesson.
  4. Same day cancellations are ineligible for make up lessons. Exceptions are at the discretion of the management. 
  5. Requests to reschedule a lesson due to sudden illness or emergency will be considered on a case-by-case basis. 
  6. Make up lessons expire 30 days after a cancelled lesson and are the customer's responsibility. Neither refund nor credit will be granted if the customer is unable to find a suitable make up time within this period, regardless of teacher availability. Expiration is 30 days from the numbered date of the original lesson and cannot be altered. Make up lessons have no cash value. Unused make up privileges do not roll-over. 
  7. Make up lessons cannot be rescheduled.
  8. All students will receive either a make up lesson or tuition credit in cases of teacher absence, weather or emergency closing. 

 
NO-CALL NO-SHOW/TARDINESS POLICY:

  • Students who no-call no-show for their lessons will not be eligible for make up under any circumstance.
  • Students who are tardy will only receive instruction for the remainder of their scheduled time.  
  • Please call if running late; if the student has not arrived after 15 minutes and no call has been received, they will be assumed a no-show, and the teacher will not be obligated to teach the remainder of the lesson. 


SUBSTITUTION POLICY: 

  • In the event of an teacher's planned or un-planned absence, Rocketeria may provide a substitute at its discretion.
  • Neither refund nor credit will be granted for teacher absence when a substitute is provided.

 

REQUIREMENTS/CONDUCT:

  • Students are required to purchase instructional materials and supplies deemed necessary by their teacher and bring them to every lesson.
  • Students are expected to progress. Lack of effort or interest may result in a student being asked to withdraw.
  • Inappropriate or disresctful behavior will not be tolerated and may be grounds for immediate dismissal without refund.

 

INCLEMENT WEATHER/EMERGENCY/SCHOOLS CLOSING: 

  • Please visit our website (www.rocketeria.biz), facebook page (www.facebook.com/rocketeria) and/or voicemail system (301-774-5690) for the most up-to-date information regarding lessons cancellation due to inclement weather, unsafe road conditions, or other unforeseen emergency. 
  • Lessons are NOT cancelled automatically when local school systems close. Our decision is independent. How Punk of us, no? 


HOLIDAY CLOSINGS: 

  • Tuition for months with holiday closings will be pro-rated:
  1. New Years Day
  2. Memorial Day
  3. Independence Day
  4. Labor Day
  5. Thanksgiving Day
  6. Christmas Eve (close at 6)
  7. Christmas  Day
  8. New Years Eve (close at 6)
