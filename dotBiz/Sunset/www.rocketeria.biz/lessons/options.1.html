WEEKLY | $41

 

Choose this option for the best value with the most flexibility; WEEKLY students may reschedule lessons, with 24 hrs. notice, (see our Music Lessons Policies for details), get a 5% family discount when 2 or more family members enroll (as WEEKLY students), get a 10% student discount on most merchandise (except for used or consignment items, sale items, printed or recorded materials, and Music & Arts rental stock purchases), and can book extra lessons at a discount with their current teacher (see ADD-ON below).

 

These are private half-hour lessons, taken on the same day and at the same time, weekly. Commitment is on a month-to-month basis. Your teacher and weekly lesson are reserved for you by way of a one-time registration fee, plus a monthly tuition. Payment for lessons is collected in full, for the month, in advance, by way of a credit card on file.

 

You pay to reserve your teacher's time whether you attend or not. You may reschedule up to 6 lessons per year, when 24-hour notice is given. Please refer to our Music Lessons Policies for exact terms and conditions.

 

ADD-ON | $31

 

ADD-ON lessons may only be purchased by WEEKLY students. They're a great way to add an extra half-hour private lesson with your current teacher, on an ad hoc basis (as their teacher's schedule permits), at a reduced rate. Weekly ADD-ON lessons may be rescheduled—up to 6 per year with 24 hrs. notice.

 

PAYG | $44

 

This is a "one-off" private half hour lesson. Choose the Pay-As-You-Go (PAYG) option when you don't want to commit to the same day and time on a weekly basis, or if you want to take lessons less frequently. Can serve as a trial lesson. To book the lesson, payment is due in full, and is non-refundable. A PAYG lesson cannot be re-scheduled. If you decide to continue as a WEEKLY student as described above, the registration fee for such is waived.

 

PAYG PACKAGES | 6-Pack $229 | 12-Pack  $349

 

Purchase a package of 6 or 12 half-hour Pay-As-You-Go lessons at a big savings. These lessons can be scheduled at your leisure, and never expire. They're perfect for new students wanting to try different teachers, or existing students needing more schedule flexibility.  Just remember that these lessons cannot be rescheduled, so book when you're sure of your availability. 

 

GROUP | $31

 

You bring us the group, we'll take it from there. 45 minute lesson. Minimum of 2 students, maximum of 6. You call the shots! Sign up for one or multiple sessions at $31 per person, per lesson. Call for schedule availability. Great for parent & child, siblings, school and "special project" groups (school talent shows, bar/bat mitzvahs, holiday family gatherings, etc.) Available for most instruments and voice, schedule permitting. 

 

JAM | $25 DETAILS

 

Available to all players (you don't have to be taking lessons here), with approval of the JAM group coach. The JAM schedule is preset. Payment is due at the time of the session. This is band coaching twice per month with a select group, in preparation for regular performances, including our annual ROCKSTRAVAGANZA at the Olney Theatre Center. More info

 

ROCK U | $299 | $199 | $99 Summer Camp DETAILS

 

A weeklong music immersion experience for middle and high schoolers who are practicing and passionate musicians with at least one to two years experience, or more. Players bond and become a band with their kind of people, participate in daily jams, improve their skill set with play tips and sound tailoring, learn and discuss rock history, put on a friends and family performance, and much more! Cost per camper is $299 for your first session, $199 for a second, $99 for a third, with a $40 per session non-refundable deposit due at time of enrollment. More info
