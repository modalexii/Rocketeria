ROCK U

ROCK U

Band Immersion Music Camp

 

Bring your passion to life! The friendly neighborhood music geeks of Rocketeria invite middle and high school practicing musicians to a week-long rock band experience. 

 

2018 SCHEDULE

 

PRE-REGISTER NOW

 

At Rock U, you’ll bond and become a band with your kind of people.

 

Participate in daily jams, learn to play by ear, improve your skill set with play tips and sound tailoring, delve into songwriting, learn and discuss rock history, put on a friends and family performance, and much more!
 

Requirements:

  • Not less than 1 year experience/training. Preferably 2 or more.
  • Must have completed 5th grade. Exceptionally skilled youngsters
    will be considered on a case-by-case basis.
  • Singers must be able to harmonize.
  • Guitarists and pianists must be able to fluently change chords
    without struggling.
  • Bassists must be able to play up and down a scale, any scale,
    with a reasonable degree of proficiency.
  • Drummers must be able to maintain a steady beat at a moderately fast tempo.

DAILY OVERVIEW:

 

Monday: Intro
Rock U overview, expectations, meet the other Rock Uers, Q&A, first jams.

 

Tuesday: First Verse 
Band and Part assignments, Songs Selection, proper rehearsals, and individual coaching!

 

Wednesday: Chorus 
Rehearsals, sectional breakdowns, and movie day!

 

Thursday: Solo 
Tone, your place in the mix, lock down song arrangements!

 

Friday: Outro 
Final rehearsal in preparation for Sunday performance for friends & family!

 

Sunday: The Gig

Bring your family and friends to a performance at a local venue, in the afternoon.
