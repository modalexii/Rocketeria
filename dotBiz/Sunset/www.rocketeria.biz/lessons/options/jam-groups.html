Jam Groups

To be in a Jam group, you should have at least two years experience in your instrument (Usually. Exceptions apply). Commitment to a Jam group is for two sessions of 75 minutes each, per month—on the first and third, or second and fourth (not every other) of your day per month. Typically, weekday sessions will begin in the 8:00 pm hour, Saturday sessions in the 3:00 pm hour. Times vary.

 

$25 per session/participant includes use of house amplifiers, drums and PA, as well as expert coaching, and performance opportunities. You don't have to be a current student, but may have to audition.

 

Participation in ROCKSTRAVAGANZA, our yearly student showcase, is open only to Jam group members. This requirement ensures a productive and satisfying experience for players (and the audience) by building in sufficient rehearsal time. 

 

Students not yet at this level may have the opportunity to participate in smaller-scale sessions in our own Room One performance space at the store—what we call ROCKcitals—by invitation from their teacher.

 

Reference the list below for openings. If you'd like to join a group, email ariana@rocketeria.biz with your schedule availability.
