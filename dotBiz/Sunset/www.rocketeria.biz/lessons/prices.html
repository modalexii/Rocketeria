 LESSONS SPECIAL

 

WEEKLY | $38

 

Choose this option for the best value, and the most flexibility; WEEKLY students may reschedule up to 6 lessons per year with 24 hrs. notice, (see our Music Lessons Policies for details), get a 5% family discount when 2 or more family members enroll (as WEEKLY students), get a 10% student discount on most merchandise (except for used or consignment items, sale items, printed or recorded materials, Music & Arts rental stock purchases.)

 

These are private half-hour lessons, taken on the same day and at the same time, weekly. Commitment is on a month-to-month basis. Your teacher and weekly lesson are reserved for you by way of a one-time registration fee, plus a monthly tuition. Payment for lessons is collected in full, for the month, in advance, by way of a credit card on file.

 

You pay to reserve your teacher's time whether you attend or not. You may reschedule up to 6 lessons per year, when 24-hour notice is given. Please refer to our Music Lessons Policies for exact terms and conditions.

 

ADD-ON | $28

 

ADD-ON lessons may only be purchased by WEEKLY students. ADD-ON lessons are great for those who would like to add an extra half-hour private lesson with their current teacher, on an ad hoc basis (as their teacher's schedule permits). ADD-ON lessons may rescheduled—up to 6 per year with 24 hrs. notice.

 

PAYG | $41

 

Choose this option when you can't commit to the same day and time on a weekly basis, or if you want to take lessons less frequently. This is a private half hour lesson, no commitment. Can serve as a trial lesson. To book the lesson, payment is due in full, and is non-refundable. A PAYG lesson cannot be re-scheduled. If you decide to continue as a WEEKLY student as described above, the registration fee for such is waived.

 

LESSONS SPECIAL | $189 - Fall 2014

 

Purchase our Weekend Warrior package of 6 PAYG lessons (see above) to schedule at your leisure, at a savings of $57! Lessons must conclude by November 30, 2014 and cannot be re-scheduled. 

 

GROUP | $28

 

You bring us the group, we'll take it from there. 45 minute lesson. Minimum of 2 students, maximum of 6. You call the shots! Sign up for one or multiple sessions at $28 per person, per lesson. Call for schedule availability. Great for parent & child, siblings, school and "special project" groups (school talent shows, bar/bat mitzvahs, holiday family gatherings, etc.) Available for most instruments and voice, schedule permitting. 

 

JAM | $25

 

This is band coaching twice per month with a select group, in preparation for regular performances, including our annual ROCKSTRAVAGANZA at the Olney Theatre Centre. 

 

Available to all players (you don't have to be taking lessons here), with approval of the JAM group coach. The JAM schedule is preset—call us for more information.
